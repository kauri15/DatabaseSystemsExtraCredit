\section*{Question 2}

You are given the following set of functional dependencies for relation
R(A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H):

\[
F = \{A \to DGH,\; BD \to EG,\; FH \to BC,\; D \to CEF\}
\]
Is the following decomposition lossless?
\[
R_1(B, E, F)
\]
\[
R_2(A, B)
\]
\[
R_3(F, G, H)
\]
\[
R_4(B, C, D, G, H)
\]

Show your work with the chase algorithm. \\\\
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

\subsection*{Solution}
Initial Chase Table:

\[
\begin{array}{c|cccccccc}
 & A & B & C & D & E & F & G & H\\
\hline
R_1 & a_1 & b & c_1 & d_1 & e & f & g_1 & h_1\\
R_2 & a & b & c_2 & d_2 & e_2 & f_2 & g_2 & h_2\\
R_3 & a_3 & b_3 & c_3 & d_3 & e_3 & f & g & h\\
R_4 & a_4 & b & c & d & e_4 & f_4 & g & h\\
\end{array}
\]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Apply $A \to DGH$

Row 2 has A:

\[
\begin{array}{c|cccccccc}
 & A & B & C & D & E & F & G & H\\
\hline
R_1 & a_1 & b & c_1 & d_1 & e & f & g_1 & h_1\\
R_2 & a & b & c_2 & d & e_2 & f_2 & g & h\\
R_3 & a_3 & b_3 & c_3 & d_3 & e_3 & f & g & h\\
R_4 & a_4 & b & c & d & e_4 & f_4 & g & h\\
\end{array}
\]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Apply $BD \to EG$

Rows 2,4 share B and D:

\[
\begin{array}{c|cccccccc}
 & A & B & C & D & E & F & G & H\\
\hline
R_1 & a_1 & b & c_1 & d_1 & e & f & g_1 & h_1\\
R_2 & a & b & c_2 & d & e & f_2 & g & h\\
R_3 & a_3 & b_3 & c_3 & d_3 & e_3 & f & g & h\\
R_4 & a_4 & b & c & d & e & f_4 & g & h\\
\end{array}
\]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Apply $FH \to BC$

Row 3 has F and H:

\[
\begin{array}{c|cccccccc}
 & A & B & C & D & E & F & G & H\\
\hline
R_1 & a_1 & b & c_1 & d_1 & e & f & g_1 & h_1\\
R_2 & a & b & c_2 & d & e & f_2 & g & h\\
R_3 & a_3 & b & c & d_3 & e_3 & f & g & h\\
R_4 & a_4 & b & c & d & e & f_4 & g & h\\
\end{array}
\]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Apply $D \to CEF$

Rows 2 and 4 share D:

\[
\begin{array}{c|cccccccc}
 & A & B & C & D & E & F & G & H\\
\hline
R_1 & a_1 & b & c_1 & d_1 & e & f & g_1 & h_1\\
R_2 & a & b & c & d & e & f & g & h\\
R_3 & a_3 & b & c & d_3 & e_3 & f & g & h\\
R_4 & a_4 & b & c & d & e & f & g & h\\
\end{array}
\]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Row 2 is now:

\[
(a,b,c,d,e,f,g,h)
\]

Row without subscripts exists.

Therefore, decomposition is \textbf{lossless}.
